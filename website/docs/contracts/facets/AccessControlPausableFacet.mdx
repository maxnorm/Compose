Contract documentation for AccessControlPausableFacet
</DocSubtitle>

<Callout type="info" title="Key Features">
- Allows dynamic pausing and unpausing of individual roles to adapt to changing requirements.
- Enforces role admin checks before pausing/unpausing to maintain access security.
- Provides explicit and structured storage for AccessControl and AccessControlPausable mechanisms.
</Callout>

## Overview

The AccessControlPausableFacet provides functionality to manage role-based access control with an additional ability to pause and unpause specific roles. This ensures dynamic and granular control over roles, allowing administrators to disable certain permissions without permanently revoking them.

## Best Practices

<Callout type="tip" title="Best Practice">
- Ensure proper access control is maintained by assigning role admins who can pause/unpause roles.
- Use &#x60;requireRoleNotPaused&#x60; in all role-based access control checks to account for paused states.
- Use clear and immutable role definitions to minimize risks during upgrades.
</Callout>

## Security Considerations

<Callout type="warning" title="Security">
Ensure only authorized admin accounts can pause or unpause roles to prevent malicious behavior. Validate all inputs like role identifiers (bytes32) to prevent unintended or malformed accesses. Use &#x60;requireRoleNotPaused&#x60; in all critical functions to protect against paused roles performing unauthorized actions. Be cautious of reentrancy attacks; avoid calling external contracts within the scope of role-dependent functions.
</Callout>
